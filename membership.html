Frequently Asked Questions

What are Diehard Dollars? ▼
Diehard Dollars (DD) are our virtual currency. Earn them through daily logins, community participation, and predictions. Spend them on badges, raffle entries, and marketplace items.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll keep your benefits until the end of your current billing period.
What is the @phillysports.com email? ▼
Diehard Pro members get a custom email address (yourname@phillysports.com) powered by Zoho Mail. It's a real email you can use anywhere!
How does the 2x coin bonus work? ▼
Premium members earn double Diehard Dollars on their daily login bonus. Free users get 10 DD base + streak bonus, while premium members get 20 DD base + streak bonus.
Do you offer refunds? ▼
We don't offer refunds for partial billing periods, but you can cancel anytime and keep benefits until your period ends. Contact us for special circumstances.
